IN TIME

| 2011 | 18+ | 1h 49min | Sci-fi/Action |

Set in a future where time is equated with wealth, Will Salas, portrayed by Justin Timberlake, lives a life of poverty with a scant day's worth of time on his clock. His fate takes a dramatic turn when he rescues Henry Hamilton, played by Matt Bomer, from time bandits, and in return, he's bestowed with a century of life. This substantial transaction, however, draws the watchful eyes of the authorities. Falsely accused of a murder he didn't commit, Will finds himself on the run, accompanied by the daughter of a tremendously affluent man, portrayed by Amanda Seyfried.
